U.S. Financial News Corpus and Metadata Audit

A financial-news corpus audit focused on source coverage, metadata quality, and which records are ready for text analysis.

Part of my UBC Sauder RA work on finance text and investment-research data sources.

GitHubWord cloudsTop publications

Short version

What it was

Which news sources, time periods, and metadata fields can support financial text analysis?

Data I touched

TDM Studio metadata partitions for U.S. business, finance, economics, and commercial news sources.

My part

I built the raw-to-analysis workflow, checked coverage by source type and year, separated metadata auditing from feature construction, and made the main corpus figures.

Where it landed

Clean metadata exports, source-coverage summaries, word clouds, topic and tone diagnostics, a summary workbook, and a notebook-style EDA report.
