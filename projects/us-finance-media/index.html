U.S. Financial News Corpus and Metadata Audit

A financial-news corpus project that starts with the boring question: what is actually in the data?

Part of my UBC Sauder RA work on finance text and investment-research data sources.

GitHubWord cloudsTop publications

Short version

What it was

Which news sources, time periods, and metadata fields are reliable enough to support financial text analysis?

Data I touched

TDM Studio metadata partitions for U.S. business, finance, economics, and commercial news sources.

My part

I built the raw-to-analysis workflow, checked coverage by source type and year, separated metadata checks from feature construction, and made figures that explain what the corpus actually contains.

Where it landed

Clean metadata exports, source coverage summaries, word clouds, topic/tone diagnostics, a summary workbook, and a notebook-style EDA report.
