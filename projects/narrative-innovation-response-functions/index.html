Narrative Innovation Response Functions

A method demo for asking whether markets respond to the part of a disclosure narrative that was not expected.

Research project in progress with Jerrold Huang; the public page only shows the method demo and non-confidential outputs.

Short version

What it was

Can event-time market responses be studied with narrative forecast errors instead of raw tone alone?

Data I touched

Public SEC 8-K text, public price data, and factor controls assembled for a method demo.

My part

I built the demo around a simple two-step design: forecast the narrative first, then study the market response to what is left over.

Where it landed

Executable notebooks, response-curve figures, attrition checks, model diagnostics, and research-design notes.
