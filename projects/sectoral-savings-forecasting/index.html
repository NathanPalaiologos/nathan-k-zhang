Canadian Sectoral Savings Forecasting

A Canadian household net-savings forecasting project built around rolling validation and benchmark comparisons.

Co-authored with Jingwen Leng, Junhao Wen, Shisheng Xiao, and Boyan Yu.

GitHubReport PDF

Short version

What it was

Can macro predictors improve household net-savings forecasts relative to persistence benchmarks?

Data I touched

Quarterly Canadian macroeconomic data from 1977 Q1 to 2019 Q4, excluding the COVID structural break.

My part

I worked on the forecasting design, wrote reusable R helpers, and compared why smaller ARIMAX models performed better in rolling validation.

Where it landed

R notebooks, a PDF report, forecast-comparison figures, RMSE tables, and reproducible environment files.
