Technology Exposure and Labor-Market Data Pipeline

Patent text, occupation tasks, and worker panels, with the checks needed before the measures enter a regression.

Built with Dr. J. Frank Li and Dr. Cheng Chen at UBC Sauder.

Short version

What it was

How can patent text and occupation task data be turned into exposure measures for worker-level empirical research?

Data I touched

Patent text and metadata, O*NET occupation measures, worker histories, and occupation crosswalks.

My part

My work covered the parquet-first pipeline, occupation-link audits, patent-task similarity jobs, and the variable construction that translates employment-sorting ideas into panel data.

Where it landed

Worker panels, exposure tables, sample funnels, occupation rankings, and documentation for replication.
