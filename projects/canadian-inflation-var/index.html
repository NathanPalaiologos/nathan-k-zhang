Canadian Inflation Forecasting and Macro Signal Evaluation

A VAR forecasting deck on Canadian CPI, macro signals, and why a bigger model is not automatically a better one.

Course project and presentation work; public materials are linked where available.

Short version

What it was

Which macro-financial indicators improve Canadian CPI forecasts, and how should model risk be explained to a policy-facing audience?

Data I touched

Canadian macro and financial indicators from public sources, including CPI, exchange-rate signals, money supply, unemployment gaps, rates, and U.S. CPI.

My part

I built the R/Quarto presentation artifact, compared forecast specifications, and converted model diagnostics into a readable macro narrative.

Where it landed

RevealJS slides, forecast charts, rolling validation figures, model comparisons, and policy-facing interpretation.
