A bit more context

I study statistics and economics at UBC, with a data science minor. The common thread is applied statistical work: what the data can support, what the model assumes, and how to explain the result without dressing it up.

Outside formal research, I run around UBC, cycle around Vancouver, watch too many films/anime, play games (sometimes), and turn side curiosities into small data projects when they refuse to remain small.
